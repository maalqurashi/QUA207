About Confidence Intervals

Mean Confidence Interval

Used when estimating a population mean from sample data. Common formulas include: • CI for mean with known population standard deviation • CI for mean with unknown population standard deviation (t-distribution)

Proportion Confidence Interval

Used when estimating a population proportion from sample data. The formula typically uses: • Sample proportion (p̂) • Standard error of proportion • Z-score based on confidence level
